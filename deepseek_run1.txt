### 2. **Predictive Capacity & Division Sliders with "Reality Check" AI**
**The Friction:** Setting capacities is guesswork. Organizers scroll through division toggles and number inputs, trying to balance seats. They often over/under-estimate.
**The Radical Solution:** Replace all individual capacity inputs with one **"Total Capacity" smart slider** that intelligently allocates seats and warns against unrealistic splits.

**Mechanics:**
*   On the "Registration & Capacity" step, the "Division Capacities" section is replaced by a visual control:
    *   A main slider for **Total Venue Capacity** (e.g., 0-200).

*   **The Predictive Logic:**
    *   The default ratio (e.g., 20% J / 30% S / 50% M) is pulled from the organizer's *historical average* for that tournament type.
    *   The system knows official rules (e.g., minimum players for prizes). It **visually greys out** portions of the slider that would create invalid divisions (e.g., trying to have 8 total players with 3 divisions).
    *   **"Reality Check" Pop-up:** If the organizer manually adjusts a division ratio to something wildly atypical (e.g., 80% Juniors), a gentle, non-blocking tooltip appears: *"Based on 142 past League Cups, the Junior division typically averages 22%.
